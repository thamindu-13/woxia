Lead Music Producer & Sound Engineer

With a Master's degree in Music Production from Berklee College of Music, Adeena brings over a decade of experience in sound engineering and electronic music production. Her unique approach to sound design has earned her multiple industry awards and collaborations with leading artists worldwide.
